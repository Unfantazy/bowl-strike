What you should know
  • Show/swipe your membership card at reception
  • You must sign in and provide proof of ID at reception
  • You must live outside of a 15km radius to be able to sign in as a visitor
  • You must remain in the company of the member whilst in the club
  • Provide acceptable evidence of age where there is any doubt under 25
